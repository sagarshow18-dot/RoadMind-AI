Frequently Asked Questions

How does RoadMind AI protect our data? expand_more

We utilize enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II compliant, ensuring your agency's information is handled with the highest security standards.

Can we integrate with existing IoT infrastructure? expand_more

Yes. RoadMind AI is designed with an API-first philosophy. We support standard MQTT and RESTful protocols to ingest data from existing traffic sensors, weather stations, and connected vehicle fleets.

What is the typical deployment timeline? expand_more

Standard cloud deployments take between 2 to 4 weeks, including initial model training on your historical data. On-premise or edge-computing solutions may require additional time for hardware calibration.
